What we're covering
Remnants of Hurricane Ida are unleashing dangerous flash floods and tornadoes across the Northeast, including New York, New Jersey and Connecticut.
States of emergency have been declared in New York City, New York state and New Jersey due to the storms.
President Biden will deliver remarks today on his administration's response to Ida, which made landfall on Sunday in Louisiana.
